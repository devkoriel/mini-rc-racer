# Implementation Backlog

This backlog is ordered by design impact, not engineering novelty.

## P0: Vertical Slice Truth

### P0.1 Stage-First Shell

- Shrink top-page chrome until the viewport dominates
- Move telemetry into the stage region
- Compress control/setup UI into a secondary dock
- Keep mobile touch ergonomics intact

Done when:

- desktop first screen is clearly game-first

### P0.2 Maple Block Density

- Add close-range suburban scale props to every sector
- Ensure curb, sidewalk, driveway, lawn, and road surfaces are distinct
- Remove any empty-field reads from normal play angles

Done when:

- one screenshot immediately reads as neighborhood RC racing

### P0.3 Chase Camera Lock

- Tune camera height, distance, FOV, lag, and look-ahead
- Keep the player car small enough to preserve scale
- Keep corners readable without rising too high

Done when:

- 10 seconds of driving feels intentional

### P0.4 Vehicle Scale and Materials

- Tune player car silhouette and material breakup
- Make parked cars clearly larger than the player car
- Improve road/curb/sidewalk contrast

Done when:

- the world consistently feels larger than the car
